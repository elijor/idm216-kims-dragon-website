Competitor Research

Kung Fu Tea

Offerings

  • Asian inspired drinks, food, and merchandise (emphasis on tea)
  • Rewards for frequent customers
  • Order online and payment
  • Delivery & pickup options
  • Customizable drinks
  • Buy and send digital gift cards
  • Limited edition drinks & merch

Strengths

  • Easy scan to pay option for quick transactions
  • Online rewards that encourage users to frequently buy
  • Customizable drinks online for size, toppings, and sugar level
  • Price transparency
  • Promotions displayed on homepage
  • Location finder

Usability & Accessibility

Accessibility

  • Mostly AA passing color contrast
  • Navigation issues for VoiceOver users
  • Ineffective button names for Voice Control users
  • Overall readable font size

Usability

  • Easy to navigate
  • Consistent design and content
  • Forgiving information architecture
  • Internet connection bug upon reopening

Key Demographics

  • College and High School Students 16-24 years old
  • People who prefer tea to coffee
  • Primarily northeastern Urban residents
  • Frequent customers

Weaknesses

  • No customizable Accessibility setting available in app
  • Homepage is cluttered and overwhelming
  • Locations lookup is clunky and awkward
  • Food lacks imagery and description

Unique Functionality

  • Scan to pay with easy tipping buttons and custom tipping options
  • Many ongoing unique promotions displayed clearly on homepage
  • Locations lookup is clunky and awkward
  • Food lacks imagery and description
